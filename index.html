Our Story

The Open Horizons Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that believes in every child's right to support and opportunity—no matter their challenges. Through community partnerships, advocacy, and direct funding, we strive to expand quality care, education, and resources across Texas and beyond. We are dedicated to creating opportunities for children with autism, congenital heart defects, and other diagnoses, empowering them to become effective, contributing members of society.


The need for our work is clear: 316 children born daily who may later be diagnosed with autism, that is 13 children born per hour. As of 2025, 1 in 31 children (about 3.2%) aged 8 years in the U.S. were diagnosed with ASD. Additionally, 2–3 million individuals in the U.S. live with congenital heart defects. These statistics drive our mission to provide essential support and resources to these communities.

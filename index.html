Our Story

Open Horizons Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that believes in every child's right to support and opportunity—no matter their challenges. Through community partnerships, advocacy, and direct funding, we strive to expand quality care, education, and resources across Texas and beyond. We are dedicated to creating opportunities for children with autism, congenital heart defects, and other diagnoses, empowering them to become effective, contributing members of society.

Our Impact
